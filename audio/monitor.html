Wireless in-ear monitoring, via Bluetooth

Read discussion on in-ear traditional systems versus Bluetooth. According to a November 2017 article, the best aptX (of the two kinds) has a latency of 35ms. A read of the Wikipedia article on aptX suggests there's more than two kinds of aptX, and the one that looks intriguing is aptX Low Latency: the wiki article mentions an end-to-end latency of 32ms over Bluetooth. A user at a forum post suggests this $15 receiver as the winning combinotion. Perhaps the combination is good, but the trasmitter has on its Amazon page "The latency of 40ms is still unacceptable for live sound monitoring. Therefore, those who plan to use it with electric paino or guitar, beware! Bluetooth technology, even with aptX Low Latency, may not suit your needs; ".

Some people have done a latency test. Additional conversations on IEM for Bluetooth is found at forums here, here, here, here, here, and here. Lists of best bluetooth products are here.
